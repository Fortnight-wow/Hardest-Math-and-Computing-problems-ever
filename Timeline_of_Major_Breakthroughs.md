# Timeline of Major Mathematical Breakthroughs

## 1637
Fermat states Fermat's Last Theorem.

## 1742
Goldbach proposes the Goldbach Conjecture.

## 1852
Four Color Problem introduced.

## 1859
Riemann publishes his paper on the zeta function.

## 1904
Poincaré formulates the Poincaré Conjecture.

## 1954
Yang and Mills introduce Yang-Mills theory.

## 1971
Stephen Cook introduces NP-completeness.

## 1976
Appel and Haken prove the Four Color Theorem.

## 1994
Andrew Wiles completes the proof of Fermat's Last Theorem.

## 2000
Clay Mathematics Institute announces the Millennium Prize Problems.

## 2002–2003
Perelman proves the Poincaré Conjecture.

## 2013
Yitang Zhang proves bounded gaps between primes.

## 2016
Maryna Viazovska solves the sphere-packing problem in dimension 8.

## 2016
Boolean Pythagorean Triples solved using SAT solvers.

## Present
Many of the most famous problems remain open.
